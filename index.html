Quick Reference

Capture from your phone

Share sheet or Home Screen → Capture shortcut. First option is Just capture — no filing decision needed; it lands in Inbox flagged unsorted.

Or file directly as Task, Vault, Spark, or Recipe. Files sync via Dropbox and route within ~3 min.

Sparks & Recipes

Spark = a link + your one-sentence reaction (permanent commonplace log).

Recipe = paste a link; scrapeable pages become full recipes, Instagram/Threads become bookmarks. Drop Osta PDFs in recipes/raw/. Use Plan This Week for a grocery list.

2/s Someday   3/a Waiting

Tab Sweep shortcuts

1/k Keep   2/s Save
3/t Make Task   4/d Done Reading

Someday Review shortcuts

1/k Keep   2/w Promote (then pick day)
x Delete   Space Skip   Esc Close

Items in Someday for 90+ days get a "consider deleting" hint.

Edge Extension (Tab Sweep)

Click the Capture to Triage extension icon on any page. Add a memo about where you left off, hit Save. Then close the tab.
